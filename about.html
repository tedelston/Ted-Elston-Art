About the Artist

My Journey

Based in the vibrant city of Belfast, I am an Irish artist exploring the world through the rich and versatile medium of oil paint. My artistic journey is defined by a fascination with light, atmosphere, and the icons that shape our cultural landscape. From the moody, rain-slicked streets of Belfast to the vibrant energy of cinematic legends, my work seeks to capture the distinct character of each subject.

I find inspiration in the eclectic mix of the everyday and the iconic and approach each canvas with the same dedication to detail and narrative. My paintings often blend traditional realism with contemporary themes.

Artist Statement

Oil painting allows me to build layers of depth and luminosity that bring a scene to life. I am particularly drawn to chiaroscuro—the contrast between light and dark, which features heavily in my urban landscapes and portraits. My portfolio is a reflection of my personal interests and surroundings. Ultimately, my goal is to create work that resonates on an emotional level, whether that creates a spark of nostalgia or a moment of quiet reflection.
