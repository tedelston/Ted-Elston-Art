Commission a Painting

Commissioned Paintings I accept a limited number of oil painting commissions. Accepted subjects include portraiture, figurative work, and concept-driven pieces. All commissions are original works produced in my own style. Replicas or direct copies are not undertaken.

Process Commissions begin with a short discussion of subject matter and references. From there, I will plan out composition, scale, and painterly approach. Updates will be provided at key stages. I accept a small number of commissions at a time to ensure focus and consistency. The process is deliberately unhurried, prioritising integrity over speed.

Commission Process

1

Initial Consultation

We discuss your vision, preferences, and requirements for the piece.

2

Proposal & Quote

I provide a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing based on your needs.

3

Creation

I begin work on your piece, providing updates throughout the process.

4

Delivery

Your finished painting is carefully packaged and delivered to you.
